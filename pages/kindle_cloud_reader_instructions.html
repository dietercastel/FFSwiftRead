Here's how to use SwiftRead on Kindle Cloud Reader Available on PRO

1. Visit Kindle Cloud Reader

2. Open a Kindle book

3. Open SwiftRead from menu or use hotkeys to auto-extract the text on the current page

4. Turn pages in your Kindle book from within SwiftRead by clicking on the "left" and "right" icons
